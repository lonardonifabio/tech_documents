AI Governance: Integrating ISO 42001

Authors: SMIIT CyberAI

This document focuses on the integration of ISO 42001, a new management system standard, with existing AI governance frameworks. It’s a practical guide for organizations seeking to implement AI systems while adhering to both ethical considerations and security best practices.
