AIGovernance International Evaluation Index (AGILEIndex) 2025

Authors: Yi Zeng, Enmeng Lu, Xiaoyang Guo, Cunqing Huangfu, Jiawei Xie, Yu Chen, Zhengqi Wang, Dongqi Liang, Gongce Cao, Jin Wang, Zizhe Ruan, Xin Guan, Ammar Younas

The AGILEIndex 2025 is an international evaluation index developed by a collaborative effort involving several key institutions focused on the governance of Artificial Intelligence. Released in July 2025, the index aims to provide a standardized framework for assessing and comparing AI governance practices across different nations. The project is spearheaded by the Center for Long-term Artificial Intelligence (CLAI), the Beijing Institute of AISafety and Governance (Beijing-AISI), the Beijing Key Laboratory of SafeAI and Superalignment, and the International Research Center for AIEthics and Governance, Institute of Automation, ChineseAcademyofSciences. The index represents a significant undertaking in establishing benchmarks for responsible AI development and deployment. The research is supported by substantial funding from the National Science and Technology Major Project of China (GrantNo.2022ZD0116202) and the Beijing Municipal Science&TechnologyCommission, highlighting the strategic importance placed on AI governance within China. The AGILEIndex website (https://agile-index.ai/) serves as the central hub for accessing the index, supporting data, and ongoing updates. The collaborative nature of the project, involving multiple research centers and institutes, underscores the complexity and multifaceted nature of AI governance challenges. The project's focus on long-term AI safety and superalignment further emphasizes the need for proactive measures to mitigate potential risks associated with advanced AI systems. The website links provide direct access to the contributing institutions, facilitating further exploration of the research and its underlying methodologies. This initiative is a critical step towards fostering a more robust and ethically aligned AI ecosystem globally.
