Italian Strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2024-2026

Authors: Agenzia per l’Italia Digitale AGID

This document outlines the Italian Strategy for Artificial Intelligence (AI) spanning 2024 to 2026, representing a national approach to harnessing the transformative power of AI technologies. The strategy addresses AI's pervasive impact across social and productive dynamics, recognizing its profound influence on sectors like education, industry, and professional activities. It acknowledges the historical roots of AI, tracing its evolution from early machine learning in the 2000s to the recent advancements in deep learning and, crucially, generative AI systems like ChatGPT developed by OpenAI. The strategy emphasizes the significant international investments fueling this technological revolution, moving beyond purely academic settings to become a widespread social phenomenon. The core focus is on leveraging AI's ability to extract value and knowledge from massive datasets, driving innovation and automation. Specifically, the document highlights the strategic importance of generative AI, exemplified by ChatGPT, in facilitating user-friendly automation processes. The Italian government's initiative aims to capitalize on these advancements to reshape industries and improve societal outcomes. The strategy’s design incorporates elements for research, public administration, enterprise support, and training initiatives, indicating a holistic approach to AI adoption. Furthermore, the document implicitly addresses the need for regulatory oversight, acknowledging the importance of monitoring the strategy’s implementation and the role of regulatory bodies. The overarching goal is to position Italy as a key player in the global AI landscape, fostering innovation and economic growth through strategic investment and development.
