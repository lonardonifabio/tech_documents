Resumes and Cover Letters

Authors: Harvard University Office of Career Services

This document, produced by the Harvard University Faculty of Arts & Sciences Office of Career Services, provides a foundational overview of resumes and cover letters. It defines a resume as a concise summary of an individual's skills, education, and experience, emphasizing its role in securing an interview. The guide stresses the importance of tailoring a resume to a specific job application, highlighting the need to showcase relevant skills while acknowledging that a resume alone is insufficient for securing employment. The document encourages users to seek assistance through resources such as the CARC or OCS Resume and Cover Letter Webinars, and the HES call-ins. It provides a basic framework for understanding the purpose and creation of effective resumes, focusing on strategic presentation and alignment with employer expectations.
