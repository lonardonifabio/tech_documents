Risk Ready Report 2025: Risk Management in Italian Listed Companies

Authors: Marsh McLennan

The 'Risk Ready Report 2025' by Marsh McLennan is the fourth edition of a study analyzing the risks faced by Italian listed companies. It utilizes a comprehensive review of financial and sustainability reports published in 2024 and 2023. The report’s primary objective is to deliver a structured overview of risk disclosure, connecting it to emerging trends and identifying areas requiring strategic attention and improvement. Specifically, the report examines the most frequently reported risks within Italian listed companies’ financial documents. It delves into the evolution of risk perception within the corporate landscape. The study categorizes risk analysis by sector, including Automotive, Entertainment, Fashion, Financial Institutions, Food and Beverage, Healthcare and Chemicals, Infrastructure, Construction and Cement, Manufacturing, Media and Communications, Power, Utilities, Real Estate, and Technology. The report analyzes key indices such as FTSE MIB, FTSE MID CAP, FTSE STAR, and FTSE SMALL CAP. The document highlights the increasing complexity of the business environment, marked by economic volatility, and emphasizes the need for a more strategic and informed approach to risk management. The report’s methodology involves a detailed assessment of disclosed risks, aiming to provide actionable insights for companies seeking to enhance their risk preparedness and strategic decision-making. The study’s findings are intended to support better risk governance and contribute to a more resilient and sustainable corporate environment. The report’s focus on disclosure and emerging trends underscores the importance of proactive risk management in today’s dynamic business landscape.
