The State of AI Talent 2025

Authors: Zeki Talent

This report, "The State of AI Talent 2025," analyzes the evolving landscape of artificial intelligence talent in 2025. It identifies key trends and predictions regarding the geographic distribution of top AI talent, highlighting a significant shift away from the United States. The report argues that the concentration of AI talent is changing, with India poised to become a consumer rather than a provider of this crucial resource, while European and Gulf States will intensify efforts to retain their existing talent pools. Furthermore, the document predicts Google's talent concentration will drive dominance in Large Language Models (LLMs). The report also examines specific industry impacts, noting that Big Pharma will likely outsource high-risk AI drug discovery and that the intersection of quantum and AI will primarily benefit established AI companies. A key finding is the increasing tendency for top AI talent to remain hidden, suggesting a competitive and potentially secretive market. The report emphasizes that access to top AI talent is now more critical than simply possessing computational power or capital. The analysis underscores the importance of talent acquisition and retention for companies seeking to innovate in the field of artificial intelligence. The document concludes with a disclaimer regarding liability, ownership, and limitations of use, reinforcing the need for careful consideration of the report's findings.
