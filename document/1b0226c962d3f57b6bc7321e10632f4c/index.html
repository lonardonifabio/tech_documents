This document, titled "AI Data Governance Checklist – A support to AI Implemenattion check List and Guide", serves as an implementation guide for establishing an AI data governance framework. It’s inspired by the "AI Assessment & Implementation Guide" (MSH 2025) and incorporates best practices from various sources, including ISO/IEC 42001, the EU AI Act, CAISO model, and frameworks for generative and agentic AI. The checklist focuses on mitigating the risks associated with poor data quality – inaccurate, incomplete, or biased data – which can negatively impact model predictions, erode trust, and lead to regulatory issues. The document provides a structured approach to data governance, emphasizing high-quality data. It includes a detailed checklist and deployment instructions. The core sections cover strategic data alignment, data availability & quality, data classification & security, ethical AI & compliance, data stewardship & lineage, and continuous data governance. The guide aims to provide a practical tool for organizations seeking to implement robust AI data governance practices, aligning with evolving regulatory landscapes and promoting responsible AI development. The document is designed to be a foundational resource for building a comprehensive AI data governance program.
