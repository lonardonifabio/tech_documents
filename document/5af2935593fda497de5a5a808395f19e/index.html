Authors: State of New South Wales through The Department of Customer Service

This document provides guidance for NSW government agencies on implementing responsible artificial intelligence (AI) practices. It emphasizes that responsible AI is a collective responsibility, extending across all levels of an agency, from executive leadership to individual users. The core purpose is to establish clear roles and accountability within agencies, aligning with the NSW AI Assessment Framework (AIAF) and leveraging ISO/IEC standards. Specifically, the document outlines how public servants should integrate these responsibilities into their performance plans, AI awareness training, and internal governance processes related to the evaluation, development, and operation of AI solutions. The guidance aims to ensure the safe and responsible use of AI, promoting a framework where everyone understands their contribution to ethical and effective AI implementation.
