Authors: Denis Panjuta, Denis Panjuta

This document provides a simplified explanation of fundamental AI algorithms geared towards a younger audience. Finally, it introduces Support Vector Machines (SVM) using the example of separating apples and oranges, explaining how SVM finds the optimal line to differentiate between data points. The overall tone and style are designed to be engaging and easy to understand for children, introducing core AI concepts in a practical and intuitive manner. The document aims to demystify AI by relating it to everyday experiences and familiar scenarios, fostering an early understanding of these technologies.
