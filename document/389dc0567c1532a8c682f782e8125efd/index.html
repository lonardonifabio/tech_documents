Authors: Matt Turck, John Wu

This document, titled "MACHINE LEARNING, ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE, AND DATA (MAD) LANDSCAPE 2021", is a comprehensive overview of the rapidly evolving data and AI ecosystem as of October 2021. Prepared by Matt Turck, John Wu, and FirstMark Capital, it categorizes key areas within the MAD space, including data resources, data services, and emerging technologies. The report delves into various segments such as data resources and APIs, data services, and the infrastructure supporting machine learning operations (ML Ops). It highlights trends in databases (Graph DBs, MPP DBs, NewSQL), data integration (Data Lakes, Warehouses, Reverse ETL), and the growing importance of data governance and access. Specific technologies and platforms are discussed, including those related to data science (Notebooks, Platforms, Data Analyst Platforms, Data Catalog), AI hardware, and applications across diverse industries like finance, healthcare, industrial, and transportation. The document also addresses critical considerations like privacy, security, and the rise of serverless computing. Furthermore, it covers key concepts like model building, feature stores, deployment & production, and data generation & labeling. The report emphasizes the increasing role of AI in analytics, search, and visualization, alongside the development of horizontal AI platforms. The overall aim is to provide a snapshot of the current state of the MAD landscape and identify key players and trends driving innovation in this dynamic field.
