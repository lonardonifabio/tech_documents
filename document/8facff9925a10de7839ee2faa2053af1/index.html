Governance of AI: A critical imperative for today’s boards

Authors: Deloitte

This document, a 2nd edition of Deloitte’s Governance of AI program, highlights the critical importance of board-level oversight in the rapidly evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence. It argues that organizations are often lagging behind the pace of technological advancement, necessitating a proactive approach to AI governance. The core message emphasizes that boards must actively bridge the gap between organizational change and technological progress, ensuring AI adoption drives performance, resilience, and aligns with ethical standards and long-term strategic vision. The document stresses the significant risks associated with poorly implemented AI, including eroded trust and reputational damage, while simultaneously showcasing the transformative potential of trustworthy AI. Deloitte’s research indicates a growing awareness and impetus for action among boards, who are recognizing the need to accelerate education and adoption. The second edition builds upon the initial findings, reinforcing the urgency of board-level engagement to navigate the complexities and maximize the benefits of AI. The document underscores the need for a strategic and responsible approach to AI governance, focusing on building trust and ensuring a positive impact on stakeholders. It’s a call to action for boards to move beyond simply acknowledging AI’s potential and to actively shape its development and deployment.
