SCALING DEEP TECH IN EUROPE THE EUROPEAN INNOVATION COUNCIL IMPACT REPORT 2025

Authors: European Innovation Council, European Innovation Council and SMEs Executive Agency (EISMEA)

This Impact Report, published in 2025 by the European Innovation Council (EIC) and the European Innovation Council and SMEs Executive Agency (EISMEA), provides a comprehensive overview of the EIC’s achievements in supporting Europe’s deep tech innovations. The report highlights the EIC’s crucial role in fostering a pipeline of over 1,350 unique innovations, alongside providing support to over 700 startups and SMEs operating across 30 countries. A key focus is on ‘high risk and disruptive innovations’ at all stages of maturity. The report details the significant investment generated, with over EUR 2.6 billion being ‘crowded in’ through the EIC’s activities. It emphasizes the EIC’s contribution to accelerating the development and commercialization of cutting-edge technologies within Europe. The document is a key resource for understanding the EIC’s impact and future strategy. It’s a strategic document for those interested in European innovation policy and deep tech investment. The report’s findings are intended to inform stakeholders and contribute to the continued growth of Europe’s deep tech ecosystem. The report’s scope includes a detailed analysis of the EIC’s activities and their measurable outcomes, demonstrating the agency’s effectiveness in driving innovation and supporting European businesses.
