AI makes people more valuable The Fearless Future: 2025 Global AI Jobs Barometer

Authors: PwC

This document, titled "AI makes people more valuable The Fearless Future: 2025 Global AI Jobs Barometer," is a report by PwC analyzing global job trends related to Artificial Intelligence. The core finding is that AI is dramatically increasing worker productivity and creating significant value for companies. The analysis, based on a dataset of nearly a billion job ads and thousands of company financial reports across six continents, reveals that industries actively utilizing AI experience a threefold increase in revenue generated per employee. Notably, productivity growth in these AI-adopting industries has nearly quadrupled since 2022, while declining in less exposed sectors. Crucially, workers possessing AI skills, particularly prompt engineering, command a substantial wage premium – 56% – compared to 25% the previous year. The report emphasizes that AI is not primarily about reducing headcount but rather augmenting worker capabilities. A key observation is that virtually all industries are expanding their AI usage, even those seemingly less directly impacted, such as mining and construction, demonstrating a widespread recognition of AI's potential. Furthermore, the pace of change in skills demanded by employers is accelerating, with occupations most exposed to AI exhibiting a 66% faster rate of change compared to those least exposed (25% last year). This highlights the evolving skill requirements in the age of AI. The report directly counters fears of widespread job losses, indicating growth in AI-related occupations and associated wage increases. The document provides a comprehensive overview of the impact of AI on the workforce and the evolving skills landscape, offering insights for businesses and workers navigating the transition.
