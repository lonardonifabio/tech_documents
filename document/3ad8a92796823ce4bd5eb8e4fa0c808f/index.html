AI in Action: Beyond Experimentation to Transform Industry

Authors: Accenture, World Economic Forum

This white paper series, published by the World Economic Forum in collaboration with Accenture, focuses on the strategic implications, opportunities, and challenges of adopting artificial intelligence (AI) across various industries. Released in January 2025, the document is part of the World Economic Forum’s AI Transformation of Industries initiative, which aims to catalyze responsible industry transformation through AI-driven innovation. The core objective is to move beyond simple experimentation and establish a framework for truly transformative change. The paper begins with a reading guide and a foreword, followed by an executive summary that outlines the key findings. The document then delves into the opportunities presented by AI adoption in 2025, examining the current status of AI adoption within different sectors. A significant portion is dedicated to exploring AI’s potential future, considering emerging trends and anticipated advancements. The paper concludes with a conclusion summarizing the key takeaways. It also includes contributor information and endnotes. The World Economic Forum emphasizes that the findings represent a collaborative process and do not necessarily reflect the views of the entire organization or its stakeholders. The initiative’s goal is to provide a comprehensive understanding of how AI can reshape industries, while acknowledging the need for responsible and strategic implementation. The document highlights the importance of moving beyond pilot projects and towards scalable, impactful AI solutions. It’s a strategic document designed to inform decision-makers and guide the development of AI strategies within organizations.
