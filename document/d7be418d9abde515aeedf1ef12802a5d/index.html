FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS AI IN FI REPORT 2025: For Banks, the AI Reckoning Is Here

Authors: Saurabh Tripathi, Stiene Riemer, Matteo Coppola, Kirsten Rulf, Jürgen Rogg, Christian Schmid, Michael Strauß

This report, titled "FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS AI IN FI REPORT 2025: For Banks, the AI Reckoning Is Here," published in May 2025 by BCG, analyzes the significant impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI), particularly Generative AI (GenAI) and Agentic AI, on the financial industry, specifically for banks. The document highlights that the arrival of GenAI and Agentic AI represents a critical inflection point, dramatically accelerating AI's influence and creating both substantial opportunities and considerable risks. It argues that banks are currently lagging in their AI adoption, with only a quarter actively using AI to strengthen their competitive advantage, primarily engaging in marginal experimentation. The report emphasizes that success in the coming years will depend on integrating AI strategy with overall business strategy, systematically prioritizing high-return use cases, and moving beyond isolated pilot projects. The core message is that banks must proactively embrace and scale AI capabilities to avoid being overtaken by digital competitors. The document suggests a shift from simply analyzing data to actively executing strategies through Agentic AI, fundamentally changing the banking landscape. The report underscores the urgency for financial institutions to move beyond cautious upgrades and isolated experiments to truly leverage AI's transformative potential. It’s a call to action for banks to strategically embed AI into their operations to maintain a competitive edge in a rapidly evolving market.
