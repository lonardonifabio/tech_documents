Proposal for a COUNCIL RECOMMENDATION for an EU Blueprint on cybersecurity crisis management

Authors: European Commission

This document outlines a proposal for a Council Recommendation establishing an EU Blueprint for cybersecurity crisis management. Driven by the Council's Conclusions of May 22, 2024, which urged a swift evaluation and revision of the existing cybersecurity Blueprint, the proposal aims to address the evolving cyber threat landscape. The core objectives include strengthening existing networks, fostering enhanced cooperation between organizations, and dismantling silos. The revised Blueprint will be grounded in established principles like proportionality, subsidiarity, complementarity, and confidentiality, extending them across the entire crisis management lifecycle.
