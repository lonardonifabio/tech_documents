Global Report Artificial Intelligence (2025)

Authors: International Data Center Authority (IDCA)

The "Global Report Artificial Intelligence (2025)" is a comprehensive analysis of the current and future state of Artificial Intelligence, produced by the International Data Center Authority (IDCA). The report aims to provide insights and understanding for individuals, governments, and organizations interested in the rapidly evolving field of AI. It covers a wide range of topics, including AI Processing, Generative AI and Foundation Models, Agentic AI and Business Applications, Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), Quantum Research and AI, AI Sovereignty and Responsibility, and AI and Digital Economies. The report also addresses industry challenges and opportunities, and includes a section on AI Readiness by Nations. A key focus is on transparency and providing resources for the development of digital economies worldwide. The document emphasizes that the findings and conclusions are solely those of the IDCA and do not represent the views of its members or other parties. The report's structure includes an Executive Summary, detailed sections on various AI topics, an appendix outlining industry challenges and opportunities, and a reference section. The report's creation involved a collaborative team of subject matter experts from the IDCA, highlighting a commitment to providing a thorough and accessible overview of the global AI landscape.
