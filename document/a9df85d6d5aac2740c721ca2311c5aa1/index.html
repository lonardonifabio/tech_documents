Artificial Intelligence Index Report 2025

The Artificial Intelligence Index Report 2025, the eighth edition of this influential report, provides a comprehensive analysis of the rapidly evolving artificial intelligence landscape. Released at a critical juncture, as AI’s impact on society, the economy, and global governance continues to grow exponentially, this report represents the most detailed iteration to date. Key additions and expansions within this edition include deep dives into the emerging trends of AI hardware, offering novel estimations of inference costs, and a thorough examination of AI publication and patenting patterns. Furthermore, the report incorporates fresh data concerning the increasing adoption of responsible AI practices by corporations, alongside broadened coverage of AI’s expanding role in both scientific research and the medical field. Established in 2017 as a derivative of the One Hundred Year Study of Artificial Intelligence, the AI Index’s core purpose has consistently been to furnish policymakers, journalists, executives, researchers, and the general public with reliable, rigorously verified, and globally sourced data. This data-driven approach is designed to empower these stakeholders to make more informed decisions regarding the development and implementation of AI technologies. Given the pervasive discussion surrounding AI – from corporate boardrooms to everyday conversations – the report’s mission is now more vital than ever. The AI Index continues to be a leader in monitoring and interpreting the most significant trends shaping the AI field, encompassing the dynamic geopolitical environment, the accelerated advancement of underlying technologies, and AI’s increasing influence across business, policy, and public life. The report’s longitudinal tracking methodology remains central to its value, providing a historical perspective on the field’s evolution.
