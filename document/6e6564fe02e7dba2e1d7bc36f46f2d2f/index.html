Introduction to Deep Learning

This course provides a foundational understanding of deep learning, targeting individuals seeking to enter the field of AI and specifically deep learning engineering. The course emphasizes practical skills, equipping learners with the knowledge to build, train, and apply fully connected deep neural networks. It addresses the growing demand for deep learning engineers and the significant career opportunities this field presents. The curriculum covers key trends driving deep learning, the architecture and implementation of neural networks, and the core parameters involved in their design. Specifically, the course delves into the mechanics of neural networks, explaining the concept of a single neuron and its relationship to linear regression and perceptrons. Learners will gain an understanding of how to build and train these networks, including vectorized neural networks and the importance of key parameters. Furthermore, the course aims to prepare participants for interviews within the AI industry, providing them with the ability to answer fundamental questions related to deep learning. The course's focus is on providing a deep understanding of the technology, moving beyond superficial descriptions and enabling learners to apply deep learning techniques to their own projects. It's designed for those wanting to develop a solid base for a career in AI, focusing on the practical application of neural networks and their underlying principles.
