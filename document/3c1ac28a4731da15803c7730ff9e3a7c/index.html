Conformity Assessments under the EU AI Act: A step-by step guide

Authors: Andreea Serban, Vasileios Rovilos, Katerina Demetzou

This document provides a comprehensive, step-by-step guide to conducting conformity assessments in relation to the EU AI Act. Updated with the final text of the AI Act, it offers practical guidance for organizations navigating the complex regulatory landscape surrounding artificial intelligence. The guide is designed to assist businesses and organizations in understanding and meeting the requirements outlined in the Act, ensuring compliance and mitigating potential risks. It details the key aspects of the AI Act, including risk categorization, technical documentation, and accountability measures. The guide emphasizes the importance of a systematic approach to conformity assessment, covering areas such as data governance, algorithmic transparency, and human oversight. Furthermore, it addresses the specific obligations imposed by the Act, such as the need for risk management systems and the requirements for documentation. The authors, Andreea Serban, Vasileios Rovilos, and Katerina Demetzou, draw upon their expertise to provide actionable insights and practical recommendations. Notably, these authors initially developed the Guide in 2023 while at the Future of Privacy Forum, highlighting the evolving nature of AI regulation. The document is a valuable resource for anyone seeking to understand and implement the EU AI Act, offering a clear and accessible explanation of its provisions. It is crucial to remember that this guide is a dynamic resource, continuously updated to reflect the latest developments in AI legislation and best practices. The guide's purpose is to support organizations in their efforts to develop and deploy AI systems responsibly and in accordance with the EU AI Act. The inclusion of a disclaimer underscores the importance of seeking legal counsel for specific compliance needs. The document's creation by the Future of Privacy Forum and OneTrust LLC further emphasizes the commitment to providing reliable and up-to-date information within the field of AI governance.
