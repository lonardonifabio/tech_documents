How to Create an AI Center of Excellence

Authors: Figure Eight

This Figure Eight eBook provides guidance on establishing an AI Center of Excellence within an organization. It highlights the increasing investment in AI initiatives and the importance of aligning business and technology expertise to drive revenue, improve customer experience, and leverage high-quality training data. The document emphasizes the critical need for patience and stakeholder buy-in, acknowledging that delivering tangible business impact from AI projects often takes longer than initially projected. According to Gartner, AI adoption is expected to reach 40% of Infrastructure and Operations teams by 2023. A key element of success is identifying and attracting internal ‘customers’ – individuals or teams who are interested in AI but lack the initial direction. These stakeholders’ journeys typically begin with the acquisition and utilization of training data. The establishment of an AI Center of Excellence is presented as a strategic approach to manage and govern AI initiatives, ensuring they align with business goals and deliver sustainable value. The document underscores the importance of a phased approach, starting with focused projects and gradually expanding AI capabilities across the organization. Ultimately, the goal is to foster a culture of innovation and experimentation around AI, enabling the organization to fully realize the potential of this transformative technology. The document advocates for a structured process that combines technical expertise with business understanding, facilitating successful AI adoption and maximizing return on investment.
