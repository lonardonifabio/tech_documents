RPA Developer Job Description

This document outlines the requirements and responsibilities for an RPA Developer role, primarily focused on utilizing UiPath for automating business processes. The role involves designing, coding, testing, and implementing automation workflows. Key responsibilities include providing solution designs to clients throughout the entire project lifecycle, from initial deployment to ongoing support. The developer is expected to possess a strong technical foundation in UiPath, including proficiency with Robots, Studio, and Orchestrator. A minimum of 1.5 years of experience specifically developing and implementing automation workflows within UiPath is required. Candidates must have a computer science or IT degree and a proven background in code development. Strong technical skills in UiPath, along with experience with workflow-based logic and the RE framework, are essential. The role demands excellent communication skills and a solid understanding of scripting languages such as VBA, VB, Java, or C#. Furthermore, experience with cognitive automation, OCR tools, AI/ML skills, and familiarity with concepts like Document Understanding, Computer Vision, AI Center, and Action Center are considered advantageous. Finally, experience with Mainframe Automation and SAP Automation would be a significant plus. The document emphasizes the importance of UiPath Advanced Developer Certification and a proactive approach to delivering technical training, demos, and support to clients.
