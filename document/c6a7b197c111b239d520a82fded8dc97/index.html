The Agentic State How Agentic AI Will Revamp 10 Functional Layers of Government and Public Administration

Authors: Luukas Ilves, Manuel Kilian, Tiago C. Peixoto, Ott Velsberg

This whitepaper, Version 1.0, published in May 2025, explores the potential of 'agentic AI' to transform government and public administration. It’s presented as an initial exploration rather than a definitive roadmap, designed to foster discussion, challenge existing assumptions, and encourage experimentation within the GovTech community. The authors, led by Luukas Ilves, aim for an iterative approach, continuously adapting and refining the whitepaper based on feedback and evolving developments in agentic AI. The document highlights the role of the Global Government Technology Centre Berlin (GGTC), established in 2024 by GovTech Campus Deutschland and the World Economic Forum. The GGTC’s core mission is to bridge national GovTech ecosystems with a global network of experts – encompassing government, industry, and academia – to translate technology into practical public value. The whitepaper emphasizes a collaborative approach, inviting contributions and engagement from anyone involved in addressing the challenges presented by agentic AI in public services. The GGTC operates with a sister center in Kyiv, Ukraine, signifying a broader international effort. The document’s purpose is to stimulate a dynamic conversation and drive innovation in the application of AI within governmental structures, anticipating a continuous evolution of the whitepaper itself through subsequent versions.
