AR Game Development for Beginners

Authors: Daniel Buckley, Morgan McKie

This book, "AR Game Development for Beginners," is a project-based course designed to introduce individuals with no prior experience to the fundamentals of Augmented Reality (AR) development using Unity. Authored by Daniel Buckley and Morgan McKie, both Unity Certified Developers and Professional Augmented Reality Developers, the book focuses on building games within the AR space. It leverages the Unity game engine and C# programming language, guiding learners through essential concepts like variables, functions, conditionals, loops, and object-oriented programming. The course specifically targets popular game genres including RPGs, idle games, Platformers, and FPS games, providing a practical approach to learning AR development. A key element of the learning experience is the promotion of Zenva's resources, including their Augmented Reality Mini-Degree program, which offers unlimited access to all courses, expert mentorship, and guided learning paths covering popular languages like Python, web development, and VR/AR. The book emphasizes a hands-on approach, encouraging readers to build games and develop in-demand skills for the AR development industry. The course is suitable for beginners and aims to equip them with the knowledge and skills necessary to create engaging AR games. The inclusion of Unity certification and the focus on current industry trends (like AR development) further enhance the book's value and relevance.
