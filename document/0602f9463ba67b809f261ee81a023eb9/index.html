The State of AI in 2021

Authors: McKinsey

This document, based on a McKinsey Global Survey conducted in December 2021, analyzes the ongoing adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI) within businesses. The survey highlights a continued growth in AI adoption, with 56% of respondents reporting adoption across at least one function, a significant increase from 50% in 2020. The primary benefits realized by companies leveraging AI in 2021 were centered around cost savings, particularly during the initial stages of the COVID-19 pandemic. However, the most successful AI adopters were distinguished by their implementation of sophisticated practices, including machine-learning operations (MLOps), coupled with efficient spending and strategic utilization of cloud technologies. A key finding was the increased focus on risk mitigation, as many companies struggled in this area. The report emphasizes that companies achieving the greatest earnings boosts from AI are not simply adopting core AI practices but are also investing in advanced techniques and proactively addressing potential risks. The document underscores the importance of a holistic approach to AI implementation, encompassing not just technology but also operational efficiency and risk management. The survey data provides valuable insights for businesses considering AI adoption and highlights the critical factors for maximizing its impact and minimizing potential challenges. The continued rise in AI adoption, coupled with the identified best practices, suggests a strong trajectory for AI's role in the business landscape.
