Change Management for Leaders and Managers

Authors: Andy Turnbull

This guide, "Change Management for Leaders and Managers," provides a comprehensive overview of managing change within organizations. It’s a 1st edition publication (© 2018) by Andy Turnbull and bookboon.com, identified by ISBN 978-87-403-2211-8. The book systematically addresses the complexities of organizational change, beginning with foundational understanding. It defines organizational change and explores the rationale behind managing it, including the various drivers and dimensions involved. A key focus is on the human element, examining the causes of stress, the impact on teams, and strategies for mobilizing people. The guide then delves into the practical aspects of implementing change, covering planning, communication, and leadership. It introduces a three-step model for transitions and the change curve, alongside an eight-stage model for leading change. Crucially, it emphasizes the importance of effective communication, highlighting the need to ‘communicate, communicate, communicate!’ and tailor messages to specific audiences. The book advocates for authentic leadership and active listening. Ultimately, it provides leaders and managers with the tools and knowledge necessary to navigate and successfully manage change initiatives within their organizations. The book’s structure is clearly organized into sections covering understanding change, implementing change, and the role of leadership in facilitating a smooth transition.
