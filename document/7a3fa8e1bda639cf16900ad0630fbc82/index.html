Responsible AI Deployment in Safety-Critical Settings: A Study on AI in Patient Monitoring

Authors: Morey, Rayo, Woods

This document excerpt details a study conducted by Morey, Rayo, and Woods investigating the empirical evaluation requirements for deploying AI in safety-critical settings, specifically within healthcare. The research addresses the growing adoption of AI in areas like digital medicine, despite ongoing debates and historical concerns regarding the introduction of new risks by digital technologies in healthcare. The study highlights a significant evaluation gap, noting the inconsistent impacts and methods used to assess AI augmentation, leading to uncertainty about the potential benefits or harms of AI deployments. The findings contribute to a more robust understanding of how AI can be effectively integrated into patient monitoring systems, ultimately improving patient safety.
