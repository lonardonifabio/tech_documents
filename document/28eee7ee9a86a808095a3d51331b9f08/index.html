AI in Action: Beyond Experimentation to Transform Industry

Authors: Accenture, World Economic Forum

This white paper series, published by the World Economic Forum in collaboration with Accenture, focuses on the strategic implications, opportunities, and challenges of adopting artificial intelligence (AI) across various industries. Released in January 2025, the document is part of the World Economic Forum’s AI Transformation of Industries initiative, which aims to drive responsible industry transformation through AI-driven innovation. The core objective is to move beyond simple experimentation and establish a framework for widespread adoption. The paper analyzes the current status of AI adoption, forecasts its potential future impact, and highlights the key considerations for businesses navigating this technological shift. It specifically addresses the need for a strategic approach, recognizing that AI’s successful implementation requires careful planning and consideration of both the benefits and potential risks. The document’s findings are based on a collaborative process endorsed by the World Economic Forum, representing a comprehensive view of the evolving landscape of AI. The initiative’s scope extends to business and operating models, emphasizing the need for a holistic understanding of how AI can reshape industries. The white paper series is intended to provide insights and guidance for organizations seeking to leverage AI effectively while mitigating potential negative consequences.
