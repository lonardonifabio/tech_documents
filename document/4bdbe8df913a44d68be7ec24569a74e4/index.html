OWASP Top 10 for LLM Apps & Gen AI Agentic Security Initiative

Authors: OWASP

This document, Version 1.0 released in February 2025, presents the OWASP Top 10 for Large Language Model (LLM) Applications and Gen AI Agentic Security Initiative. It focuses on identifying and mitigating security threats specifically targeting applications built around Generative AI and Agentic AI systems. The document serves as a foundational resource for developers, security professionals, and anyone involved in the design, development, and deployment of Gen AI applications. It outlines key vulnerabilities and provides guidance on how to address them. The core of the document is based on the established OWASP Top 10 framework, adapted to the unique risks associated with LLMs and Agentic AI. Specifically, it details threats related to prompt injection, data poisoning, model manipulation, and other vulnerabilities. The document includes a reference architecture for Agentic AI, a threat model, and a red teaming guide. It emphasizes the importance of proactive security measures throughout the entire lifecycle of an LLM application.
