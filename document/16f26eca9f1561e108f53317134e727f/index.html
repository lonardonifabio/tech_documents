Reviews of 'Applied AI'

Authors: Stephen Strauss, Jack Chua, Rachel Thomas, Jeff Pulver, Alex Stein

This document excerpt consists of five positive reviews of the book 'Applied AI'. The reviews highlight the book's accessibility, practicality, and relevance for business leaders and executives seeking to understand and implement artificial intelligence within their organizations. Stephen Strauss, Head of Sales Enablement and Insights at PayPal, praises the authors' depth of expertise and engaging approach to a complex topic. Jack Chua, Director of Data Science at Expedia, emphasizes the book's role as a 'playbook' for executives embarking on automation journeys, focusing on providing foundational knowledge. Rachel Thomas, a deep learning researcher and educator, commends the book's practical guidance, particularly its attention to critical issues such as AI safety, ethics, diversity, and inclusion within the industry. Jeff Pulver, Co-Founder of V onage and MoNage, describes the book as a 'perfect primer' for understanding enterprise AI implications. Finally, Alex Stein, Sr. Di, considers 'Applied AI' a 'must read' for business leaders aiming to remain competitive in the rapidly evolving field of artificial intelligence. Collectively, the reviews underscore the book's value as a clear, accessible, and strategically relevant resource for anyone involved in the adoption and management of AI technologies. The reviews suggest a focus on practical application, ethical considerations, and the importance of diversity and inclusion, reflecting a growing awareness of these issues within the AI community.
