The overall goal is to establish a framework for responsible development and deployment of general-purpose AI models, ensuring compliance with the AI Act. The collaborative approach, involving multiple Working Groups, suggests a comprehensive and nuanced strategy for addressing the complex challenges posed by general-purpose AI.
