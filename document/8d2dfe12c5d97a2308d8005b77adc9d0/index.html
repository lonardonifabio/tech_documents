AI Playbook for Cities: Harnessing the Potential of Artificial Intelligence

Authors: Shri Hardeep Singh Puri, Shri Kaushal Kishore, Shri Amitabh Kant, Shri Manoj Joshi, Shri K Rajaraman, Shri Kunal Kumar

This document, the ‘AI Playbook for Cities,’ serves as a comprehensive guide for Indian cities seeking to leverage the transformative potential of Artificial Intelligence. Developed by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MoHUA) and its associated agencies, the playbook provides a structured approach to AI implementation, targeting urban practitioners and policymakers. It begins with an introduction to AI and its relevance to urban challenges. The core of the playbook is a step-by-step toolkit, outlining a process for problem identification, prioritization, solution identification, procurement, deployment, and impact assessment. Specifically, it guides users through key stages: first, identifying the critical issues facing their city; second, prioritizing these issues based on their potential for AI-driven solutions; third, exploring methods for sourcing and testing AI solutions; fourth, detailing the procurement and scaling up of AI projects; fifth, outlining the implementation and operational aspects of these projects; and finally, emphasizing the importance of measuring outcomes, benefits, and overall impact. The playbook aims to facilitate a practical and responsible approach to AI adoption, ensuring that cities can effectively utilize this technology to improve quality of life, enhance connectivity, and deliver tangible benefits to their citizens. The document is designed to be a resource for those involved in urban planning and development, offering a framework for integrating AI into city operations and strategic decision-making. It emphasizes a phased approach, recognizing the complexities involved in deploying AI solutions within a dynamic urban environment. The playbook’s ultimate goal is to empower cities to become smarter, more efficient, and more responsive to the needs of their residents.
