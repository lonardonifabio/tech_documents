GenAI in Payments: The Impact Is Real

Authors: Rohan Panjwani, Neema Aggarwal, Apoorv Jain

This white paper, published in June 2025, examines the significant impact of Generative AI (GenAI) within the payments industry. It highlights the transformative potential unlocked by the widespread accessibility of Large Language Models like ChatGPT, following its public launch in November 2022. The document argues that businesses are moving beyond isolated experimentation with GenAI and predictive AI, towards comprehensive, end-to-end workflow redesigns that deliver measurable business value. A key theme is the shift from siloed use cases to integrated solutions, emphasizing the combined power of GenAI, predictive AI, and traditional automation. The paper cites examples of financial institutions, such as RBC, which has publicly committed to achieving $700 million to $1 billion in enterprise value through AI investments by 2027. RBC's CEO, Dave McKay, articulated this strategy, emphasizing the ability of GenAI to scale powerful capabilities across a broader range of operations. The document underscores the urgency for businesses to adopt a holistic approach to GenAI implementation, recognizing its potential to fundamentally reshape payment processes and drive substantial value. It suggests a move away from simply trying out a few AI tools to creating entirely new, optimized workflows. The paper implicitly addresses the challenges of previous AI deployments, where many organizations struggled to realize tangible returns due to a lack of strategic alignment and integration. It positions GenAI as a catalyst for significant operational improvements and revenue generation within the payments sector, aligning with the broader trend of AI adoption across various industries.
