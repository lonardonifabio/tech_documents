Machine Learning Cheat Sheet

Authors: soulmachine

This document is a concise 'cheat sheet' designed to provide a quick reference for classical equations, diagrams, and tricks commonly found in machine learning. It was created by Soul Machine and aims to simplify the understanding of complex mathematical formulas and concepts within the field. The primary goal is to address the common issues of confusing notation and omitted steps often found in traditional machine learning textbooks. Specifically, the cheat sheet tackles the problem of ambiguous symbols, which can be represented as sets, random variables, or matrices, leading to reader confusion. To combat this, the document strives for standardization of symbols, clearly defining their usage throughout. Furthermore, it attempts to retain crucial intermediate steps in mathematical derivations, preventing readers from getting lost during the process. By focusing on clarity and completeness, the cheat sheet intends to accelerate learning and improve comprehension of fundamental machine learning principles. The document is licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 Unported (CC BY-SA 3.0) license, encouraging attribution and sharing of the material. It serves as a valuable resource for students, researchers, and practitioners seeking a streamlined overview of core machine learning concepts and equations. The content is structured to facilitate rapid recall and understanding, making it a practical tool for anyone involved in machine learning.
