What AI Needs to Succeed

This report, titled "What AI Needs to Succeed," analyzes the current state of AI adoption within enterprises, drawing upon a survey of 1,620 business leaders conducted for the 2025 Process Optimization Report. The core argument centers around the critical role of Process Intelligence (PI) in unlocking the full potential of AI investments. The document highlights that while a significant majority (99%) of surveyed organizations are deploying some form of AI, and a substantial 73% are seeing positive ROI, these deployments are largely focused on relatively simple use cases like boosting developer productivity through GenAI and implementing customer support chatbots. The report emphasizes that the current AI landscape is still in its early stages, with many organizations not fully leveraging AI's capabilities due to a lack of deep process understanding and business context. It identifies key areas of AI deployment, including IT security concerns, finance and shared services investment mandates, and a nascent foothold in supply chain operations, particularly within warehouses. The document stresses that true AI success hinges on integrating process intelligence, suggesting that organizations need to move beyond basic AI implementations and develop a more holistic understanding of their processes to maximize the value of their AI investments. The survey data provides a snapshot of the enterprise's current approach to AI, revealing a trend towards automation and generative AI, but also underscores the need for strategic process alignment to achieve significant and sustainable returns.
