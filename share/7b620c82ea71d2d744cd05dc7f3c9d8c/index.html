Authors: C.B. Griesinger, V. Reina, D. Panidis, H. Chassaigne

This report, titled "AI evidence pathway for operationalising trustworthy AI in health: Ontology of ethical principles", details a framework for integrating ethical considerations into the development and deployment of trustworthy AI within the healthcare sector. The document outlines an ontology that translates ethical principles into tangible, translational, and fundamental concepts. It focuses on providing a structured approach to operationalizing trustworthy AI, addressing the complexities of ensuring ethical behavior in AI systems used in health. The work is conducted by researchers at the Joint Research Centre (JRC) of the European Commission, specifically targeting the development of a robust pathway for aligning AI systems with established ethical guidelines. The report emphasizes the need for a clear and actionable methodology for organizations seeking to implement trustworthy AI solutions in healthcare.
