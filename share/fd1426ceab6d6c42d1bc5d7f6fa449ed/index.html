Procurement of AI Community Model contractual clauses for the public procurement of High -Risk AI

Authors: European Commission

This document, titled 'Procurement of AI Community Model contractual clauses for the public procurement of High -Risk AI' (MCC -AI-High -Risk), is a working document produced by the European Commission to assist public administrations in the procurement of Artificial Intelligence (AI) solutions. Specifically, it focuses on High-Risk AI systems, aligning with the requirements of the upcoming AI Act. The document serves as a guide, outlining model clauses designed to be incorporated into procurement processes. It emphasizes that users are solely responsible for its application in public procurement. The core of the document centers around defining key terms, notably 'AI System,' which is defined as a machine-based system exhibiting autonomy and adaptability, capable of generating outputs based on inferred objectives. The document's purpose is to standardize and facilitate the responsible procurement of AI, ensuring alignment with regulatory frameworks and best practices.
