Strategic Approach to Artificial Intelligence

This document outlines a strategic approach to the development and implementation of Artificial Intelligence (AI) within Denmark, published in December 2024. The core objective is to foster responsible AI practices and establish Denmark as a global leader in the field. The strategy focuses on four key initiatives designed to drive this transformation. Firstly, the establishment of the Digital Taskforce on Artificial Intelligence, tasked with positioning the Danish public sector as world-leading in AI innovation. Secondly, the creation of a Centre for Artificial Intelligence in Society, intended to facilitate research and understanding of AI's societal implications. Thirdly, the development of a secure platform specifically for creating transparent Danish language models, reflecting a commitment to localized AI solutions. Finally, the document highlights the importance of secure development practices.
