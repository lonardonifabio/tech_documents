United Nations Conference on Trade and Development Technology and Innovation Report 2021

Authors: United Nations Conference on Trade and Development

The United Nations Conference on Trade and Development’s (UNCTAD) Technology and Innovation Report 2021, titled ‘Catching Technological Waves: Innovation with Equity,’ examines the critical intersection of technological advancements and equitable development. The report delves into how emerging technologies, including new and emerging technologies, are reshaping global trade and investment patterns. It specifically addresses the challenges and opportunities associated with harnessing these technologies to foster inclusive growth and reduce inequalities. The document highlights the importance of policy interventions and strategic investments to ensure that the benefits of technological innovation are widely distributed, rather than concentrated in the hands of a few. It analyzes the impact of these technologies on various sectors, including trade, investment, and sustainable development. The report emphasizes the need for governments and international organizations to proactively manage the risks and maximize the potential of technological disruption. Furthermore, it explores the role of innovation ecosystems, including research and development, entrepreneurship, and knowledge sharing, in driving sustainable economic growth. The document also addresses the digital divide and the importance of bridging the gap in access to technology and digital skills. The UNCTAD’s STI Policy Section, which conducted the policy-oriented analytical work, focuses on the impact of innovation and new and emerging technologies on sustainable development. The report’s findings and recommendations are intended to inform policy decisions and guide investments towards a more inclusive and sustainable future. The UNCTAD’s approach is rooted in promoting trade and development, and this report contributes to that mission by analyzing the technological landscape and identifying strategies for leveraging innovation for broader societal benefit. The report’s data and analysis are crucial for understanding the evolving dynamics of the global economy and for shaping policies that promote innovation and equitable growth.
