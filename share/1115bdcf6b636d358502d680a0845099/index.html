Intelligent Automation in HR Services and Solutions

Authors: Steve Goldberg, Dr. Tom Reuner

This report, produced by HfS Research in December 2016, examines the strategic approaches to Intelligent Automation (IA) within the HR services and solutions landscape. It distinguishes between the strategies employed by services providers and enterprise software companies. Services providers typically focus on leveraging IA to decouple labor arbitrage from the broader service delivery value chain, assessing whether increased staff capacity or cognitive computing horsepower can address customer business problems. Conversely, enterprise software companies prioritize IA centered around cognitive computing technologies such as algorithms, natural language processing, and deep learning. This approach enables the development of chatbots that enhance employee self-service and introduces predictive tools into practical applications. The report highlights how Artificial Intelligence, through these IA capabilities, is fundamentally changing the HR service delivery model.
