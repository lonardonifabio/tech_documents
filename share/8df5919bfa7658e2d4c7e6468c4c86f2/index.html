Artificial Intelligence in Capital Markets: Use Cases, Risks, and Challenges

Authors: International Organization of Securities Commissions

This consultation report, produced by the International Organization of Securities Commissions (IOSCO), focuses on the rapidly evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence (AI) within capital markets. The document addresses key use cases of AI in this sector, alongside a thorough examination of the associated risks and challenges. IOSCO recognizes the significant potential of AI to transform capital markets but emphasizes the need for robust regulatory oversight to mitigate potential harms. The report aims to inform discussions and shape future policy decisions related to AI adoption in securities markets. It specifically welcomes feedback from a broad range of stakeholders, including financial market participants, AI developers, academics, researchers, public policy experts, and other interested parties. The consultation process is designed to gather diverse perspectives and ensure that regulatory frameworks are informed by practical experience and cutting-edge research. IOSCO’s goal is to foster a responsible and innovative environment for AI in capital markets, balancing the benefits of technological advancement with the need to protect investors and maintain market integrity. The deadline for submitting public comments is April 11, 2025, and all comments will be made publicly available. The report’s scope includes not only current applications of AI but also anticipates future developments and potential disruptions. The document highlights the importance of ongoing monitoring and adaptation as AI technology continues to advance. It underscores the need for collaboration between regulators, industry participants, and technology providers to ensure a smooth and secure transition.
