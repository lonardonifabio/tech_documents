General-Purpose AI Code of Practice

Authors: Signatories

The "General-Purpose AI Code of Practice" is a final version released in July 2025, designed to enhance the functioning of the internal market and promote the adoption of human-centric and trustworthy artificial intelligence (AI) within the European Union. Its primary goal is to mitigate the potential harms associated with AI, aligning with the principles outlined in the AI Act. Specifically, the Code acts as a guiding document for AI model providers seeking to demonstrate compliance with Articles 53 and 55 of the AI Act. Crucially, adherence to the Code does not automatically guarantee compliance with the AI Act, serving instead as a voluntary framework. The Code's objectives include facilitating the AI Office's ability to assess compliance among providers who choose to utilize it. The document is a foundational element in the EU's strategy for regulating AI, aiming to foster innovation while minimizing potential negative consequences.
