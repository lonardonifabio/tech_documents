AI Continent Action Plan

Authors: European Commission

This document, COM(2025) 165 final, is a Communication from the European Commission to the European Parliament, the Council, the European Economic and Social Committee, and the Committee of the Regions. It outlines a strategic plan, titled the "AI Continent Action Plan," aimed at solidifying the European Union's position as a global leader in Artificial Intelligence. The core objective is to transform the EU into a leading "AI continent." The Communication emphasizes the EU's commitment to proactively shaping the future of AI, recognizing the transformative potential of AI across key economic sectors. It acknowledges that AI is already impacting industries and addresses the need for swift and decisive policy action to maintain competitiveness, uphold democratic values, and safeguard cultural diversity. A key theme is the development of 'trustworthy and human-centric AI,' which is considered vital for both economic growth and the protection of fundamental rights. The document highlights the dynamic nature of the AI landscape within the EU, fueled by ongoing research, emerging technologies, and a robust ecosystem of startups and scaleups. The European Union recognizes the intense global competition for AI leadership and underscores the importance of continued innovation and strategic action.
