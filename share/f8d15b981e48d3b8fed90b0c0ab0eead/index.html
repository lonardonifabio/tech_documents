This document, titled "AI makes people more valuable The Fearless Future: 2025 Global AI Jobs Barometer," is a report by PwC analyzing global job trends related to Artificial Intelligence. The core finding is that AI is dramatically increasing worker productivity and creating significant value for companies. The analysis, based on a dataset of nearly a billion job ads and thousands of company financial reports across six continents, reveals that industries actively utilizing AI experience a threefold increase in revenue generated per employee. Notably, productivity growth in these AI-adopting industries has nearly quadrupled since 2022, while declining in less exposed sectors.
