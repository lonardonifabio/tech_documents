REGOLAMENTO (UE) 2024/1689

This document is the full text of REGOLAMENTO (UE) 2024/1689, a European Union legislative act adopted by the European Parliament and the Council. It establishes harmonized rules concerning artificial intelligence (AI). Specifically, it amends several existing EU regulations and directives, including (CE) No. 300/2008, (UE) No. 167/2013, (UE) No. 168/2013, (UE) 2018/858, (UE) 2018/1139, (UE) 2019/2144, directives 2014/90/UE, (UE) 2016/797, and (UE) 2020/1828. The primary objective of this regulation is to enhance the functioning of the internal market by creating a uniform legal framework, particularly for the development, market entry, deployment, and use of AI systems within the European Union. It aligns with the values of the Union, promoting an anthropocentric and reliable AI while ensuring a high level of protection for health, safety, and fundamental rights. The legislative process followed a standard ordinary procedure, incorporating feedback from the Committee of the Regions, the European Central Bank, and the European Economic and Social Committee.
