The safe and effective use of AI in education Leadership toolkit video transcripts

This document excerpt details the transcripts for a leadership toolkit video series focused on the safe and effective implementation of Artificial Intelligence (AI) within the education sector. The series, released in June 2025, addresses the transformative potential of AI across various aspects of school and college operations, including administrative processes, teaching, professional development (CPD), inclusion, and personalized learning. The core theme revolves around a considered and safe approach to adopting AI, prioritizing human oversight and ethical considerations. The video content is structured around four key videos, each exploring a specific facet of AI integration. Video 1 provides an introduction, highlighting the significant technological changes driven by AI and its potential to enhance educational practices. Video 2 focuses on an audit of the current situation, assessing readiness for AI adoption. Video 3 delves into the safety aspects of AI, examining limitations of AI systems, data and intellectual property concerns, safeguarding protocols, and safe tool selection. It also addresses curriculum and skills development. Video 3.2 explores the opportunities presented by AI, including principles of digital technology use, reducing workload for teachers and leaders, and supporting personalized learning, alongside support for business, operations, and support staff. Finally, Video 3.3 concentrates on embedding AI within a digital strategy, covering student use of AI, adopting effective practices, and the importance of CPD. Video 3.4 provides guidance from the Department for Education. The overall document emphasizes a human-centered approach to AI integration, recognizing the need for careful planning, ethical safeguards, and ongoing professional development to maximize the benefits of AI in education while mitigating potential risks. The toolkit is designed to equip educational leaders with the knowledge and resources necessary to navigate the evolving landscape of AI in education responsibly and effectively.
