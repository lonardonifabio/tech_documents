GPT-4.1 Prompting Guide

Authors: Unknown

This document provides a prompting guide specifically designed for the GPT-4.1 family of models, which represents an advancement over GPT-4o in areas like coding, instruction following, and long context understanding. The guide compiles key prompting techniques based on extensive internal testing, aimed at helping developers fully utilize the enhanced capabilities of GPT-4.1. While established best practices such as providing context examples and crafting clear instructions remain relevant, a crucial shift is observed: GPT-4.1 demonstrates a greater adherence to instructions and a more literal interpretation of prompts compared to previous GPT models. This increased steerability means that precise, unambiguous prompts are particularly effective in guiding the model's behavior. The guide emphasizes the importance of iterative evaluation and experimentation, acknowledging the inherent nondeterministic nature of large language models and the empirical nature of AI engineering. The document highlights that a single, clearly defined sentence can often be sufficient to correct deviations in model behavior. It advocates for a proactive approach to prompt engineering, encouraging developers to build informative evaluations and continuously refine their prompts to achieve optimal results. The guide serves as a valuable resource for anyone seeking to maximize the performance of GPT-4.1, offering practical examples and a strategic approach to prompt design.
