Deep Learning from Scratch: Building with Python from First Principles

Authors: Seth Weidman

This book, "Deep Learning from Scratch: Building with Python from First Principles," authored by Seth Weidman, provides a comprehensive guide to understanding and implementing deep learning concepts from the ground up. It emphasizes building models from fundamental principles, allowing readers to gain a deep understanding of the underlying mathematics and algorithms. The book focuses on using Python as the primary programming language, offering practical examples and exercises to solidify knowledge. It covers topics such as neural networks, backpropagation, convolutional neural networks, recurrent neural networks, and more. The approach is designed to be accessible to readers with a basic understanding of Python and linear algebra. The book is structured to build a strong foundation in deep learning, enabling readers to not just use existing frameworks but to truly understand how they work. It's a hands-on approach, encouraging experimentation and a deeper comprehension of the core concepts. The book is published by O'Reilly Media and includes a revision history and details about the company's resources and contact information. It's a valuable resource for those seeking a thorough understanding of deep learning principles and implementation.
