These chatbots can address questions about product availability, store locations, and other relevant information, ultimately driving immediate sales. The document implicitly addresses the shift in advertising strategies necessitated by the loss of third-party cookies and the need for businesses to adapt by focusing on direct customer data and sophisticated targeting techniques.
