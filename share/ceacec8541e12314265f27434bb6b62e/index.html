Scalable AI Incident Classification

This work is a collaboration with Simon Mylius, who developed a tool using an LLM to classify reported incidents according to risk taxonomies and visualizes the analysis through interactive dashboards. The raw incident reports from the full AIID dataset have been processed by an LLM-based tool, classifying risks according to the MIT AI Risk Taxonomies and harm severities according to a severity rating scale based on the CSET Taxonomy of AI Harm.

Key Concepts:

  • Scalable AI Incident Classification
  • Collaboration with GovAI
  • Proof-of-concept for Automated Analysis
