AI & Partners employs a multi-faceted approach, combining direct technical support, policy engagement, global advocacy, grassroots professional services, regulatory interventions, and participation in industry groups like AI Commons. The organization’s strategy involves a ‘best-efforts’ basis for predictions and analysis. It highlights the importance of safeguarding user rights and mitigating potential systemic risks associated with general-purpose AI models. The report’s creation reflects a proactive stance, anticipating the challenges and opportunities presented by the EU AI Act and the broader adoption of AI technologies. The inclusion of frequently asked questions and explanations of key concepts demonstrates a commitment to transparency and education.
