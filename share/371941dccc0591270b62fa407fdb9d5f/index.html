This document, published by AI & Partners, provides an overview of the European AI Office and its regulatory efforts concerning the EU AI Act, scheduled for July 2025. The report highlights AI & Partners' mission to defend and extend digital rights for users globally, particularly those at risk due to the potential harms of unregulated AI. The organization focuses on a multi-faceted approach, combining technical support, policy engagement, global advocacy, grassroots professional services, and regulatory interventions. They address the risks associated with unknown AI adoption, including confusion, risk, and reputational damage, by offering tools for identification, monitoring, and governance of AI systems. The report emphasizes the importance of proactive management and responsible AI practices to ensure long-term trust and innovation. AI & Partners actively participates in industry groups like AI Commons, demonstrating a commitment to collaborative solutions and shaping the future of AI regulation. The document serves as a foundational piece for understanding the organization's strategic direction and its role in navigating the evolving landscape of AI governance within the European Union and beyond. It underscores the need for a human-centric approach to AI development and deployment, prioritizing ethical considerations and user rights.
