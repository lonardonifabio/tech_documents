KENYA ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE STRATEGY 2025-2030

The "Kenya Artificial Intelligence Strategy 2025-2030" is a comprehensive document outlining Kenya’s approach to leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) for national development. Released in March 2025, the strategy focuses on establishing a robust AI ecosystem within the country, addressing key concerns and capitalizing on the strategic importance of AI. The document begins with an executive summary, foreword, and acknowledgments, followed by an introduction that defines key AI terms and provides background information. It then details the rationale for developing an AI strategy, specifically highlighting AI concerns within Kenya and the strategic value of AI for the nation. The methodology employed in creating the strategy is thoroughly explained, including a literature review, primary data collection, and stakeholder engagement processes. A crucial section analyzes the environmental factors surrounding AI adoption in Kenya, considering societal implications and technological advancements. The strategy delves into defining emerging technologies and their relationship with AI, ultimately advocating for a proactive approach to AI development and implementation. The document’s core objective is to guide Kenya’s journey towards becoming a regional leader in AI innovation and adoption, fostering economic growth, and addressing societal challenges through responsible AI deployment. The strategy emphasizes a multi-faceted approach, encompassing research and development, skills development, ethical considerations, and collaboration across various sectors. It’s a strategic document designed to shape Kenya’s future in the age of AI, aiming to maximize the benefits while mitigating potential risks. The document’s scope extends beyond simply adopting AI; it’s about building a sustainable and impactful AI ecosystem. The strategy’s timeline, 2025-2030, indicates a medium-term vision, suggesting a phased approach to implementation and continuous evaluation. The document’s structure and detailed analysis demonstrate a serious and well-considered plan for Kenya’s AI future.

Key Concepts:

  • Development of an AI strategy for Kenya by 2030.
  • Identification of key AI concerns within Kenya.
  • Strategic importance of AI to Kenya's development.
  • Kenya’s AI future – a collective vision for AI development.
  • Establishing a robust AI ecosystem to support Kenya’s technological leadership.
  • Promoting responsible AI use and ethical standards in AI development and deployment.
  • Government prioritization of digital services for efficient service delivery.
  • Expansion of high-speed internet infrastructure through fibre optic networks.
  • Deployment of the National Optic Fibre Network Backhaul Initiative to connect sub-counties.
  • Adoption of cloud services for AI application development and data management.
