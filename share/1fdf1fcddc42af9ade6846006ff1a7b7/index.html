Second Draft General-Purpose AI Code of Practice

Authors: Chairs and Vice-Chairs of the four Working Groups

This document presents the second draft of the General-Purpose AI Code of Practice, developed under the AI Act. The draft is intended for participants in the Working Groups and observers of the Code of Practice Plenary, who are invited to submit written feedback by 15 January 2025 via a dedicated survey. Crucially, the document emphasizes that incorporating feedback at this stage does not guarantee inclusion in the final Code, as significant revisions and evaluation will occur in subsequent drafts. The Code specifically addresses key considerations for providers of general-purpose AI models and those with systemic risk, aligning with Chapter V of the AI Act. Four Working Groups are collaborating closely to achieve this. The document highlights the iterative nature of the development process, acknowledging that changes may occur even if elements remain unchanged in this draft. The overall goal is to establish a framework for responsible development and deployment of general-purpose AI models, ensuring compliance with the AI Act. The collaborative approach, involving multiple Working Groups, suggests a comprehensive and nuanced strategy for addressing the complex challenges posed by general-purpose AI.
