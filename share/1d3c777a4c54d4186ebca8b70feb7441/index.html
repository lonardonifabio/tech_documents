AI Policy Template

Authors: Responsible AI Institute

This document presents an AI Policy Template developed by the Responsible AI Institute, dated June 2024. It serves as a foundational document for organizations seeking to establish responsible AI management practices. The template includes various provisions that require careful review and potential modification based on the specific circumstances of an organization's business and its use of artificial intelligence technologies. A key disclaimer emphasizes that the policy is not intended to satisfy all legal requirements in every jurisdiction and advises users to consult with legal counsel for guidance on applicable laws and regulations. Given the rapid evolution of AI, the document stresses the need for regular policy revisions (at least annually) to ensure alignment with current standards and legal requirements.
