This research paper introduces "The AI Risk Repository," a comprehensive resource designed to address the significant lack of shared understanding surrounding the risks associated with Artificial Intelligence (AI). Recognizing that this ambiguity hinders effective discussion, research, and response strategies, the authors have created a living database containing 1612 risks derived from 65 taxonomies. The repository utilizes a systematic review process, combining structured classifications with expert consultation, to develop its taxonomies. The core of the repository is a searchable database accessible through a dedicated website and online spreadsheets, allowing for flexible filtering and ongoing updates.
