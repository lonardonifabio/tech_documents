Tackle the data science process step-by-step

Authors: Brian Godsey, Manning

This book provides a comprehensive guide to the data science process, structured around the three key phases involved in a typical data science project lifecycle. The second phase, building the product, involves planning, execution, and utilizing statistical tools and software based on the insights gained during preparation. Finally, the third phase, finishing, encompasses delivering the product, incorporating feedback, making revisions, providing ongoing support, and ultimately wrapping up the project.
