Open Source Technology in the Age of AI

Authors: McKinsey, Mozilla Foundation, Patrick J. McGovern Foundation

This report, published in April 2025, examines the growing trend of organizations utilizing open source technology in conjunction with the increasing deployment of generative AI across various business functions. The research, a collaboration between McKinsey, the Mozilla Foundation, and the Patrick J. McGovern Foundation, highlights a significant shift towards open source solutions driven by the widespread adoption of AI. Key findings indicate that over 50% of respondents are leveraging open source in data, models, and tools within their tech stacks. Technical maturity and developer experience are strong drivers of open source adoption, with the technology, media, and telecommunications sectors leading the way at 70% usage. Experienced AI developers are 40% more likely to utilize open source technologies. The report also reveals that organizations are primarily relying on open source tools developed by major technology companies like Meta (Llama) and Google (Gemma). A significant portion of respondents express high satisfaction with open source AI models, citing performance and ease of use as primary reasons. Notably, open source demonstrates a clear advantage in cost benefits compared to proprietary tools, which often offer faster time to value. The research underscores a strategic move towards open source as a core component of AI infrastructure, particularly as organizations seek to balance innovation with cost-effectiveness and control. The survey data provides valuable insights for businesses and developers navigating the evolving landscape of AI technology.

Key Concepts:

  • Increasing adoption of open source AI solutions due to generative AI deployment.
  • Open source AI usage is prevalent across data, models, and tools within tech stacks.
  • Technical maturity and developer experience drive open source AI adoption, particularly in the technology, media, and telecommunications sectors.
  • Open source offers cost benefits compared to proprietary tools, while proprietary tools provide faster time to value.
  • Organizations' reliance on tech stacks created by major corporations (Google, Meta, Microsoft)
  • Popularity of open source models like Llama, Gemma, Mistral, and Phi
  • Importance of open source AI for competitive advantage
  • Growth of the open source AI model landscape
  • Performance parity with proprietary models
  • Community-driven AI development
