Artificial Intelligence Controls Matrix (AICM) - Release Date 2025

Authors: CSA, AI Controls Working Group

The Artificial Intelligence Controls Matrix (AICM) is a vendor-agnostic framework designed to facilitate the secure and responsible development, implementation, and use of AI technologies within cloud environments. Released on July 10, 2025, the AICM is built upon the Cloud Control Matrix (CCM) and leverages existing standards from organizations like ISO, NIST, and BSI. It provides a structured approach to assessing and managing AI-specific risks, ultimately contributing to the creation of trustworthy AI systems. The framework consists of 18 security domains encompassing 243 control objectives. Developed by the AI Controls Working Group, the AICM aims to offer a robust, understandable, and measurable outcome for organizations seeking to align their AI practices with international standards.
