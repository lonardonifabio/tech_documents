Key Concepts:

  • Agentic Oversight Framework (AOF): A framework for utilizing AI agents in regulated financial services, emphasizing reasoning, adaptation, and goal-directed actions.
  • AI Agent Capabilities: The ability of AI agents to reason, adapt to context, and take actions with minimal human intervention.
  • Automated Compliance: The use of AI agents to speed up manual reviews in KYC and sanctions screening processes.
  • Agentic AI is characterized by goal-oriented behavior and autonomy, allowing it to operate independently.
  • The ability for an agent to plan, adapt, and make decisions based on environmental feedback is a core feature.
  • Comparing agentic AI with rules-based systems and machine learning highlights the differences in adaptability and agency.
