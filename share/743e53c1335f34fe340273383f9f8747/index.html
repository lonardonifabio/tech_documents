This document, the "AI Ecosystem Brief 2025 Edition Portugal," is a publication by AI & Partners, focusing on the evolving landscape of artificial intelligence and its impact on digital rights. The report highlights AI & Partners' mission to defend and extend the digital rights of users globally, particularly those at risk due to the potential harms of uncontrolled AI development. The organization employs a multi-faceted approach, combining direct technical support, policy engagement, global advocacy, grassroots professional services, regulatory interventions, and participation in industry groups like AI Commons. A core belief of AI & Partners is that AI should be a tool for unlocking potential rather than causing harm, reflecting a proactive stance against the negative consequences often associated with losing control of AI systems. The report emphasizes the importance of responsible AI development and deployment, advocating for safeguards to mitigate risks and ensure ethical considerations are prioritized. AI & Partners actively engages in shaping the future of AI through strategic collaborations and advocacy efforts. The document is authored by a team of experts including legal professionals, technologists, and researchers, demonstrating a comprehensive understanding of the challenges and opportunities presented by AI. Contact information and a website URL are provided for further information and engagement.

Key Concepts:

  • AI should unlock potential, not cause harm.

  • Establishment of a National AI Authority for oversight and coordination.

  • Importance of compliance for AI developers operating in Portugal.
  • Safeguarding fundamental rights and public trust through AI regulation.
  • Integration of AI across education levels (INCoDe 2030, Upskill programs)
  • Audit of government algorithms for compliance with Law 27/2021 principles
  • Safe environments for real-world data testing and model validation (ZLTs)
