This OECD Artificial Intelligence Paper presents a proposed common reporting framework designed to standardize the reporting of AI incidents globally. The core objective is to establish a benchmark for stakeholders across various jurisdictions and sectors, facilitating a more consistent understanding of AI-related risks and incidents. The framework prioritizes flexibility, allowing countries to adapt reporting approaches to align with their specific domestic policies and regulations. Key Concepts:

  • A common reporting approach for AI incidents across jurisdictions.
