A Brief Introduction to Neural Networks

Authors: David Kriesel

This document, "A Brief Introduction to Neural Networks," is a foundational overview of neural networks, originally prepared as part of a University of Bonn seminar in 2005. The primary goals were to provide a comprehensive explanation of neural networks and to enhance knowledge of LaTeX. The document underwent significant revisions, expanding from an initial 40 pages to a downloadable format, driven by its popularity online (www.dkriesel.com). David Kriesel, the author, personally created all illustrations using XYpic within LaTeX, aiming for clarity and memorability for readers new to the subject. The text emphasizes a practical approach, reflecting the author's desired learning experience. The document's creation was motivated by a desire to make neural network concepts accessible to a broad audience. The author's intention was to create a resource that would be both informative and engaging, fostering a deeper understanding of neural networks. The document's success as a download indicates a strong demand for accessible learning materials on this topic. It represents an early effort to disseminate knowledge about neural networks, highlighting the author's dedication to creating a user-friendly resource.
