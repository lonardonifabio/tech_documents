A Global Survey of Enterprise Adoption and Value ROI of Gen AI

Authors: Google Cloud, National Research Group

This report presents a comprehensive benchmark of the impact of generative AI (Gen AI) on business and financial performance, based on a survey of 2,500 senior leaders of global enterprises with revenues exceeding $10 million. The survey was conducted by Google Cloud and National Research Group. The study focuses on organizations actively utilizing Gen AI in their product offerings. Key findings are presented across four chapters: a benchmark of the global business impact of Gen AI, trends in top-performing organizations leveraging Gen AI, strategies for reinvesting in innovation, and 7 key takeaways for business executives. The report targets senior executives including IT Directors, Heads of Innovation, CISOs, CDOs, CSOS, COOs, Directors of Digital Strategy, VPs of IT, CFOs, CMOs, CTOs, and CIOs. The methodology details the survey's scope and the selection criteria for participants, highlighting the focus on organizations actively employing Gen AI. The report aims to provide actionable insights for business leaders seeking to understand and capitalize on the transformative potential of Gen AI.

Key Concepts:

  • A global survey of enterprise adoption and the ROI of Gen AI.
  • The report is based on a survey of 2,500 senior leaders of global enterprises.
  • Gen AI's impact on business and financial performance is benchmarked.
  • Connecting financial and technology drivers for strategic alignment.
  • Escaping the 'pilot purgatory' to achieve tangible business impact.
  • Measuring business value through direct financial impact and realized business benefits.
  • Accelerated time-to-value for organizations leveraging Gen AI.
  • Organizations are seeing significant revenue growth through the adoption of Gen AI.
  • A strategic approach to integrating Gen AI is crucial for maximizing ROI and long-term growth.
  • Gen AI can be a powerful revenue generator and delivers results within months of launch.
