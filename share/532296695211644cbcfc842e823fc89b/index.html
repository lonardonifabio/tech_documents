Digital Transformation Agency AI Model Clauses Version 2.0

Authors: Digital Transformation Agency

This document, Version 2.0, produced by the Digital Transformation Agency of Australia, outlines key clauses related to Artificial Intelligence (AI) usage. It serves as a framework for agreements, specifically addressing the use of AI by sellers. The core principle established is that any seller's utilization of AI requires prior approval from the buyer. Furthermore, the document emphasizes the seller's responsibility to verify the accuracy of the AI model being deployed. The Digital Transformation Agency acknowledges potential inaccuracies and advises against relying solely on this document for critical commercial decisions. The agency is dedicated to ensuring accessibility of its information, offering support via email at communications@dta.gov.au. This document represents a foundational element within the agency's broader efforts to govern and manage AI applications, aligning with broader government initiatives focused on responsible technology adoption.
