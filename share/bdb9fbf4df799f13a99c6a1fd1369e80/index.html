LightRAG with Neo4j

Authors: Giorgi Kurtanidze

LightRAG is an enhanced version of Graph RAG, designed to accelerate knowledge retrieval and reduce operational costs. It leverages the strengths of both graph databases (Neo4j) and vector databases (embeddings) to provide a dual-level retrieval system. The core concept is to combine structured reasoning through graph relationships with the flexibility of text search using embeddings. This approach is particularly useful in scenarios like building AI agents for complex systems, such as bug-tracking systems.
