AI Policy Review and Governance

Authors: Unknown

This document excerpt outlines key requirements for establishing and maintaining an effective AI policy within an organization. It focuses on governance and control, specifically addressing the development and use of AI systems. The core elements detailed are centered around documentation, approval, and ongoing review. Firstly, it mandates the creation and documentation of an AI policy, requiring formal management approval and a clear definition of AI development and usage guidelines. Supporting this, the document stresses the need for evidence of approval, including management approval records and policy review and revision logs. Secondly, it emphasizes alignment with existing organizational policies, demanding an assessment of potential impacts and interdependencies. This involves identifying policies affected by AI objectives, conducting policy impact assessments, and updating relevant policies to ensure alignment. Crucially, the document mandates a regular review process for the AI policy, specifying planned intervals and additional reviews triggered by significant changes. The review process must be documented, and records of these reviews, including approval records for policy modifications, are essential. The overall goal is to ensure the AI policy remains relevant, adequate, and effective throughout its lifecycle, reflecting evolving organizational objectives and technological advancements. The document highlights a proactive approach to governance, prioritizing continuous monitoring and adaptation to maintain compliance and maximize the benefits of AI adoption. The focus on documentation, approval, and regular review demonstrates a commitment to responsible AI development and deployment, mitigating potential risks and fostering trust.

Key Concepts:

  • Documentation of an AI policy for development or use of AI systems.
  • Assessment of policy interdependencies related to AI objectives.
  • Regular review and updating of the AI policy to maintain its suitability and effectiveness.
  • Defining and documenting AI system operational requirements
  • Regular review and updates of operational procedures based on system performance
  • Management approval of AI system operations
  • AI system technical documentation for various audiences (users, partners, regulatory authorities)
