A Closer Look at the Existing Risks of Generative AI

This paper analyzes 499 publicly reported incidents of Generative AI failures and maps them to the harms they precipitate. The authors construct a taxonomy specifically for Generative AI failures, describing what harms are reported, how they arose, and who they impact. They find that most reported incidents are caused by use-related issues but bring harm to parties beyond the end user(s) of the Generative AI system at fault.
